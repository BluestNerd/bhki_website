About BHKi

Our story, vision, and mission

Our Story

Conceptualized in 2019 at ICIPE during a coffee break, BHKi was born in Nairobi with the idea to create a community where students could interact with experts in the field, get resources, and share on all matters pertaining to bioinformatics.

Our Vision

To be a vivacious, well-coordinated and enthusiastic hub that will develop bioinformatics skills set through creating awareness, collaborations and capacity development.

Our Mission

Bridging the gap between well-established bioinformaticians and aspiring ones through peer training and mentorship so as to enhance collaborations to foster quality scientific research and innovations and promote the sharing of data, skills, and codes.
